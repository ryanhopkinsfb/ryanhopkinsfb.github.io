What can you do?

Clearly, you play a vital role in helping us combat modern slavery and human trafficking.
After all, you'll often have direct contact with plenty of our suppliers and third parties who provide us with the many goods and services that are used right across our business.
As a minimum, you should:
Familiarise yourself with our rules and policies
Look out for signs of modern slavery and human trafficking as you do your job
Conduct due diligence on all suppliers and third parties before they are engaged
Tell us about any concerns that you have or if you see anything suspicious
