Summary
Here's a reminder of your responsibilities in relation to modern slavery:
Read all our policies and processes - make sure you know how to reduce the risk of modern slavery and human trafficking across our operations
Look out for signs of modern slavery - such as someone with few personal possessions, inadequate clothing for the season, lack of identification and other physical signs (e.g. malnourishment)
Always conduct due diligence on all suppliers and third parties that provide raw materials, goods and services to our organisation - make sure that you 'know the chain'
Never confront anyone suspected of involvement in modern slavery - you may put potential victims at greater risk and valuable evidence may be lost
If you have concerns or see anything suspicious in our supply chain, report it to your line manager, your local HR team or via our confidential reporting hotline
Proceed to the Affirmation and Assessment when you are ready
